  • Privacy Policy for Daily Saga

    Last Updated: 12/19/2023
  • Thank you for using Daily Saga. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app, which does not collect or process any identifiable personal data. We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable privacy laws.
  • 1. Information Collection:
  • Our app does not collect any personal identifiable information from users. We do not request or store any data such as names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • 2. Usage Information:
  • We may collect non-personal information about your app usage, such as device information, app activity, and general analytics. This information is collected in an anonymous and aggregated form and is used solely for improving the functionality and user experience of our app.
  • 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies:
  • Our app does not use cookies or similar technologies for tracking or collecting user information.
  • 4. Device Local Storage:
  • Our app uses the device's local storage, this is required for our app to function properly. The local storage is used to store the following: user preferences, and user created data such as quests and attributes. This data is only stored locally and is never processed outside of the app.
  • 5. Data Security:
  • We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of any information transmitted through our app. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
  • 6. Children's Privacy:
  • Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it from our records.
  • 7. Changes to This Policy:
  • We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the updated policy here. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
  • 8. Contact Us:
  • If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at szechdevelop@gmail.com.
